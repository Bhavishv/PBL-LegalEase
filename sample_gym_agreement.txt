GYM MEMBERSHIP AGREEMENT

This Gym Membership Agreement ("Agreement") is made and entered into on this 6th day of April, 2026, by and between UltraFit Gym ("Gym") and the undersigned individual ("Member").

1. MEMBERSHIP TERM AND AUTOMATIC RENEWAL
The initial term of this membership is 12 months. This Agreement shall automatically renew for successive 12-month periods on each anniversary of the Effective Date, unless Member provides written notice of non-renewal at least sixty (60) days prior to the end of the then-current term. Failure to provide such notice will result in the immediate charge of the annual membership fee for the next term.

2. CANCELLATION POLICY AND EARLY TERMINATION FEES
Members wishing to cancel their membership before the end of the term must provide a written cancellation request. In the event of early termination for any reason other than a documented medical emergency, Member shall be liable for an early termination fee equal to 100% of the remaining balance of the contract. Additionally, a $150 administrative processing fee will be applied to all cancellation requests.

3. PAYMENT AND LATE FEES
Membership dues are processed on the 1st of every month via automatic ACH withdrawal. Any payment not received within three (3) days of the due date will incur a late fee of $50 plus interest at a rate of 18% per annum, compounded monthly.

4. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY AND WAIVER
Member acknowledges that the use of Gym facilities involves inherent risks of injury. Member hereby waives any and all claims against Gym, its employees, and owners for any injury, loss, or damage arising out of the use of the equipment or participation in fitness classes, even if caused by the negligence of the Gym or its staff.

5. DATA PRIVACY AND THIRD-PARTY SHARING
Gym reserves the right to capture, store, and analyze Member's biometric data, including heart rate and body composition, for "internal research purposes." Gym may share Member's contact information, fitness progress, and demographic data with third-party insurance providers, marketing partners, and data aggregators without obtaining further consent from the Member.

6. BINDING ARBITRATION AND CLASS ACTION WAIVER
Any dispute, claim, or controversy arising out of or relating to this Agreement shall be settled by binding arbitration in accordance with the rules of the American Arbitration Association. Member expressly waives the right to a trial by jury. Furthermore, any arbitration under this Agreement shall take place on an individual basis; class arbitrations and class actions are not permitted.

7. FACILITY RULES AND ACCESS
Gym reserves the right to change its hours of operation, available equipment, and class schedules at its sole discretion without prior notice to Members. No refunds or credits will be issued for "temporary" closures of sections of the facility for maintenance or renovation.

8. ENTIRE AGREEMENT
This document represents the entire agreement between the parties and supersedes all prior oral or written representations. No amendments shall be valid unless in writing and signed by both parties.
